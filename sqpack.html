What is it?

sqpack is a Squish message base packer.

What does it need?

Operating systems:
  • All supported platforms.
Dependencies:
  • sqpack-current requires fidoconfig-current, smapi-current and huskybse-current from the same day.
  • sqpack-1.1.0 requires fidoconfig 0.10 and smapi 1.6.4 and huskybse 0.1.
  • sqpack-1.0.3 requires fidoconfig 0.9 and smapi 1.6.2.
