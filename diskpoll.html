What is it?

diskpoll exchanges mail and files between FTN systems that have mutual access to the filesystems where their outbound directories are stored. For use with sysop points, downlinks in the same LAN as their uplink, gateways etc.

What does it need?

Operating systems:
  • All supported platforms except 16 bit DOS.
  • Requires a C++ compiler.
Dependencies:
  • None.
