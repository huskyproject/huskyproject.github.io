What is it?

mpost posts text files to FidoNet echos and netmail areas.

What does it need?

Operating systems:
  • All supported platforms.
Dependencies:
  • mpost-current requires smapi-current and huskybse-current from the same day.
  • mpost 2.0-stable requires smapi 1.6.4-stable
  • mpost 1.22 requires smapi 1.6.2.
