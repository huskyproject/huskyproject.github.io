Download Husky!

Current Husky is available from http://husky.sourceforge.net/cvs2. Do not use it without good reason.

To get stable Husky versions, you can browse to the individual entires of each programs (see below in the navigation bar), or you can directly access our download page at http://sourceforge.net/projects/husky/.

If you are using Debian GNU/Linux take a look at our Debian-Page.. You could install the Husky-Programs using the normal and easy Debian-way (apt-get, dselect,...).

If you are using BeOS take a look at Walther: Fidonet for BeOS page.
